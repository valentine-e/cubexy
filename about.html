Smart Living, Beautifully Built.

Modular ADUs for modern lifestyles — crafted with precision, designed for comfort.
Cubexy is a design-forward ADU company on a mission to redefine small space living. We create modern, modular homes that combine smart functionality with architectural beauty. Whether it’s a cozy guesthouse, a rental unit, or a remote office — we build it fast, affordably, and sustainably.

  • Custom ADU Design

    Tailored to your lifestyle, space, and budget — from sleek A-frames to urban micro-lofts.

  • Permitting & Compliance Support

    We navigate local zoning laws and permit requirements so you don’t have to.

  • Full-Service Build & Delivery

    Our prefabricated units are built off-site and installed with minimal disruption to your property.

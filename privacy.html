Privacy Policy

Cubexy ("we", "our", or "us") values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, share, and protect your personal information when you visit our website, communicate with us, or use our services.

  • 1. Information We Collect
    We collect the following types of information:

    • Personal Information:

      • Name

      • Email address

      • Phone number

      • Property address

      • Billing and payment details

      • Project preferences and consultation notes

    • Technical Information:

      • IP address

      • Browser type

      • Device type

      • Website usage data (via cookies and analytics tools)

  • 2. How We Use Your Information

    • We use your information to:

      • Respond to inquiries and schedule consultations

      • Provide services and deliver ADU projects

      • Process payments and generate invoices

      • Send project updates and support communications

      • Improve our website and customer experience

      • Comply with legal obligations

      We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

  • 3. Sharing Your Information

    We may share your information with:

    • Trusted partners (e.g. contractors, architects, engineers) involved in your project

    • Government authorities or permitting agencies as required

    • Service providers (e.g. email platforms, payment processors) under confidentiality agreements

    All third-party service providers are required to use your data only for the services they perform on our behalf.

  • 4. Your Choices

    You can:

    • Opt out of marketing emails at any time via the unsubscribe link

    • Request access to or deletion of your personal data

    • Ask us to correct inaccurate or outdated information

    To make a request, contact us at privacy@cubexy.com

  • 5. Data Security

    We use reasonable physical, technical, and administrative safeguards to protect your information from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse.

  • 6. Cookies & Tracking

    Our website uses cookies and similar technologies to improve functionality and analyze site usage. You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings.

  • 7. Children’s Privacy

    Cubexy’s services are not intended for children under 16. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

  • 8. International Users

    If you are accessing our services from outside [Your Country], be aware that your data may be processed and stored in accordance with local laws.

  • 9. Updates to This Policy

    We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. We will post any changes on this page with the updated effective date.

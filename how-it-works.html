How it works

Cubexy makes it easy to add stylish, functional living space to your property. We handle the details, you enjoy the results.

  • 1: Free Consultation

    We start with a conversation.
    Tell us about your space, your goals, and your vision. Our team will assess your property remotely or in person and suggest the best ADU options for your needs and zoning requirements.

    Book a Free Consultation

  • 2: Design & Quote

    Tailored design, clear pricing.
    Once we understand your space, we craft a design that suits your style and budget — whether it’s a compact office, a rental suite, or a guest house. You’ll receive a detailed visual layout and a transparent, fixed quote.

  • 3: Permits & Paperwork

    We will help you handling the red tape.
    Navigating permits can be frustrating — that’s why we support you at every step. We work with you to ensure your ADU meets all zoning, building, and energy code requirements.

  • 4: Build & Delivery

    Precision-built, delivered to your site.
    Your unit is constructed off-site in our controlled facility to ensure top-quality craftsmanship and efficiency. Once ready, it’s transported to your location and installed with minimal disruption.

  • 5: Move-In Ready

    Turn the key and settle in.
    From hookups to final inspections, we complete every detail so your ADU is ready to use immediately. Whether it's for living, renting, or working — your Cubexy unit is good to go.

  • Cubexy Official Contractor License

    This certificate verifies that the licensee has met all requirements to operate as an official ADU contractor under the standards and oversight of Cubexy Modular ADU Company.

    This license grants the holder official authorization to design, construct, and install modular Accessory Dwelling Units (ADUs) in accordance with state and municipal building codes, zoning laws, and Cubexy’s quality standards.  It serves as formal recognition of the licensee’s ability to uphold Cubexy’s commitment to craftsmanship, safety, and customer trust throughout the entire ADU development process.
